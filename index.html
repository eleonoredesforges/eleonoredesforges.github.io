What is Rakuten Ichiba ?

Currently the biggest e-commerce platform in Japan and the largest marketplace in the world, Rakuten Ichiba started in 1997.

With over ¥3 trillion of domestic merchandise sales, 47 000 merchants in 2018, and 100 millions consumers (80% of Japanese population), business is increasing year-over-year.

Rakuten expanded his business worldwide in US, France and Brazil. However, even with global expansion, Rakuten business is mainly focused on Japanese e-commerce market. 

Which products can you sell ?

Rakuten Ichiba have several product categories where you can almost send everything you want, but top sellings are health & food, clothing and electronics in Japan. International brands - especially clothing and apparel - are very popular.

If you can’t buy an an international brand product in Japan, Rakuten generally have it. Otherwise, if you can purchase it in a local store, Rakuten sell it at a lower price.

Rakuten Ichiba offer opportunities for foreign companies. 

For whom and why ?

Rakuten customers include many personas : rich, upper-class, low-budget, the elderly and young people.

Then, Rakuten has a points system linked to credit card, integrated in many stores throughout the country. This system allows to push loyal customers to purchase more and more products on Rakuten.

That’s the reason why Rakuten became the largest e-commerce marketplace in Japan : creating loyalty, attracting famous brands, having the most interesting prices and access to unavailable products inside the country. 

How we sell ?

Two options are available.

We set you up directly on Rakuten Ichiba

¤ Creating a store by your own is almost impossible if you are not a Japanese company or entity. Many difficults requirements are needed for the screening process with a long registration with papers and materials submission to Rakuten Ichiba, like owning a Japanese bank account or fill in your Japanese company's details.

After being screened and rigourous background checks, storefront creation is required on Rakuten Merchant Server, and takes around 1 month and a half (with preparation and screening).

¤ Another solution is to have Rakuten Japan Entry to act on your company’s behalf using our legal status to open the Rakuten store under your company’s name. This is slightly easier and quicker than the full-scale approach. In addition, we can also customize your storefront, optimize it, localize your content and boost traffic with digital marketing actions.

We sell your products on our store

Otherwise, you can choose the another option,
selling your products on our own Rakuten storefront.
With this solution, you avoid long-process and material
requirements, and we are in charge to put your items on
our platform.

However, this solution does not provide your own store,
and requires a monthly payment. If you want to sell some
products of your brand in Japan, it might be the right solution.
Otherwise, if your will is investing Japanese market and put a
large items choice in your own store, choosing the first solution
would be better. 

 Conclusion

Personally we feel for purposes of initial entry the smart money is setting up within our store.
Then if your brand/products are successful you can decide if it is worth having your own store or getting Rakuten Japan Entry to be the legal owner of your proxy store.
